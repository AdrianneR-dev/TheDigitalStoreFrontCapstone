About

Salvg Beauty was established by founder and CEO, Adrianne Rhodes in 2021 in Edwards, Mississippi. Adrianne grew frustrated with the high price of quality kombucha skincare and decided to formulate her own and price under $20 USD. At Salvg Beauty, we believe that quality skincare should be affordable. There are so many pollutants in the air - so much so that your skincare routine should be able to combat those pollutants throughout the day. Anti-pollution skincare may not be mainstream in the market yet but it will be in high demand soon.
