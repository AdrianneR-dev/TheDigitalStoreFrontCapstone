  • Unused & Unopened Products: To receive a full refund back to your original payment method, items must be unopened, unused, and in their original packaging.

  1. Email our customer care team at [Insert Email Address] with your Order Number and the reason for your return.

If your handmade lotions or soaps arrive damaged, melted due to extreme transit weather, or if we made a mistake with your order, we want to make it right immediately!

Please email us at [Insert Email Address] within 48 hours of delivery with:
